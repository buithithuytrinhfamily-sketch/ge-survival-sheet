### Slide 01 — WHO IS IN CONTROL?
Every candle is a **tug-of-war** between Buyers and Sellers. 💪
- Long **gold** body = Buyers are winning big.
- Long **red** body = Sellers are winning big.
- A **long wick (tail)** = one side pushed hard but got shoved back — no clear winner yet.

Just look at *long body vs long tail* and you'll know who's stronger.

👉 **Save this image** as your quick-check before every trade!

### Slide 02 — CANDLESTICK BREAKOUTS
Price just "broke out"? Don't jump in yet — that's how you get trapped. ⚠️
Think of a **moving bus**: don't chase it, wait for it to **come back to the stop** (a retest around the ~62% zone), then hop on.
- **Weak** breakout (no pullback) → DON'T enter.
- **Strong** breakout (pulls back, retests, then pushes up) → much safer entry.

### Slide 03 — THE FOREX PIERCING CANDLESTICK
After a **red** (down) candle, a **bullish** candle closes **past the halfway point (50%)** of that red body. 🔄
Meaning: **the losing team just won back more than half the score in the 2nd half** → momentum is flipping to the Buyers.
The deeper it pierces, the stronger the reversal signal.

### Slide 04 — THE CONFIRMATION CANDLESTICK
Some patterns are **sure of themselves** — others **need extra proof** first. 🔍
- **Engulfing:** confirms itself right inside the pattern → usable straight away.
- **Hammer / Shooting Star:** only a "hint" — wait for **one more candle** to confirm before you act.

Don't trust the very first candle too fast.

### Slide 05 — THE TRIPLE CANDLESTICKS
**Three candles tell one story** about price about to turn. 📖
- **Top row** = "sky clearing up" → **bullish reversal** (Morning Star, Three White Soldiers...).
- **Bottom row** = "storm rolling in" → **bearish reversal** (Evening Star, Three Black Crows...).

Spot a full 3-candle set and the market is "changing the wind."

👉 **Save this** so you never have to memorize it — just open it when you need it 💾
